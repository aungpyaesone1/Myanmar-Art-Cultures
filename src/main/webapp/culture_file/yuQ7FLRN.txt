<p class="p1">Myanmar traditional music are based from the sounds made from instruments grouped in the following five categories.<br />1) Kyee Kyee or Bronze made instruments such as gongs.</p>
<p class="p1">2) Kyoo Kyoo or String instruments such as Harp.</p>
<p class="p1">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p1">3) Tha Yey Tha Yey or Hide instruments like drums.</p>
<p class="p1">4) Lei Lei or Wind instruments such as Flute.</p>
<p class="p1">5) Let Khok Let Khok or Clappers, such as the Bamboo clappers.</p>
<p class="p1">In today's modern musical troupe, piano, accordion, trumpet, guitars, organs and other instruments are also included to compose songs.</p>
