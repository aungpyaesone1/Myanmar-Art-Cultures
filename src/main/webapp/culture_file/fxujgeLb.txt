<p class="p1">Bark Painting has been a new generation of wood designing. The manufacturers of Bark Paintings usually use the bark from large trees and old trees. The barks of the tree are taken off and dried in the sun. After drying, the bark paintings are done in Myanmar Traditional way.</p>
<p class="p1">Some nails, glue are used to make hold of the paintings and are polished to give a brighter look. Artificial colors are used in creating the paintings so that it can be more attractive. Bark Paintings are now in the local market and also becoming an export product.</p>
