<p><strong>Wood carving</strong> is a form of <a title="Woodworking" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Woodworking">woodworking</a> by means of a cutting tool (knife) in one hand or a <a title="Chisel" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chisel">chisel</a> by two hands or with one hand on a chisel and one hand on a mallet, resulting in a wooden figure or <a title="Figurine" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Figurine">figurine</a>, or in the <a title="Sculpture" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sculpture">sculptural</a> ornamentation of a wooden object.</p>
